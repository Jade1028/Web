Masachika finally recognizes Maria as the girl from his memories—his first love.  After a memorable summer, Masachika and his classmates enter their second semester at Seiren Private Academy! Knowing how much the school festival influences the presidential elections, Masachika encourages Alya to get involved. But he becomes uncomfortable when the distance between her and her classmates shrinks.  “Are you…jealous?”  As Alya smiles mischievously at him, his heart skips a beat.
